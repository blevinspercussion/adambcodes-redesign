I have played drums and percussion since 1988 and have worked as a drum and percussion instructor since 1996. In that time I have travelled all over the United States playing with a multitude of bands in a wide variety of musical styles.

In 2012 I began learning the basics of coding and computer science, beginning with basic Perl scripting. After learning the basics of coding with Perl, I moved on to basic Python and Javascript. After self-studying off and on for several years I delved into the world of front-end web development. I honed my front-end skills with the help of The Odin Project and by building out my own projects. I also started a Youtube Channel to help teach the basics of coding.

I currently teach private drum lessons and tutor coding from my home in the Chattanooga area where I also teach percussion classes at several public schools in the area. I am open to freelance web development as well as full-time work as a front-end developer. Be sure to use my contact page if you would like to hire me!
